Project Brief

Where River
Meets
Community.

The Urban Infill project reimagines an underutilised waterfront site on Old Tangalle Road as a vibrant cultural hub — stitching together the urban fabric, the river edge, and the community through architecture.

A distinctive pedestrian bridge connects the building to the opposite riverbank, becoming a landmark in itself. The ground floor opens onto a semi-outdoor cultural hall and café, while upper floors frame dramatic views of the river. Exposed stone walls, deep overhanging roofs, and wrought-iron railings root the design in its Sri Lankan context.
