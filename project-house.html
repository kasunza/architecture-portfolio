Project Brief

Where Home
Meets
Nature.

The Private Residence project explores the relationship between domestic space and landscape. Set on a generous plot, the design creates a home that flows seamlessly between sheltered interior life and the natural garden setting.

A central courtyard organises the plan, bringing light and greenery into the heart of the house. The pitched roof references vernacular Sri Lankan forms while the material palette — exposed concrete, timber, and natural stone — grounds the building in its context.
