Project Brief

Industrial
Architecture
Reimagined.

The Factory Complex project explores the intersection of industrial efficiency and human-centered design. This large-scale manufacturing facility challenges the traditional perception of industrial architecture as purely utilitarian.

The design integrates natural light through carefully positioned skylights and clerestory windows, creating a work environment that is both productive and humane. The administrative block is articulated as a distinct volume, bridging the language of industry and corporate architecture.
